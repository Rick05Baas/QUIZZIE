Information About The Game

Assetto Corsa ( Italian For “Race Setup”) is a sim racing game developed by Kunos Simulazioni. He started in 2010. 3 years later was assetto Corsa released thought the steam early access program on 8 November 2013 and officially left Early Access as final release version on 19 December 2014. On 3 June 2015 was announced that Assetto Corsa would come to Xbox One and Playstation 4 in 2016, Assetto Corsa Competizione On 12 September 2018 Assetto Corsa Competizione is In early access and was fully released on 29 may 2019. On 11 march 2020 Publisher 505 games announced ports for PS4 and Xbox One which was released on 23 June 2020. PS5 and Xbox Series X/S versions were announced at Gamescom 2021 and were released on 24 2022. Assetto Corsa Competizione has 22 circuits and 43 different cars.The successful Assetta Corsa competizione is now the official organizer of the Total 24 Hours of Spa.
