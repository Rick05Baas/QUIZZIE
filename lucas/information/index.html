Information About The Game

The first Gran Turismo was released on 23 December 1997 by Polys Entertainment. The producer Kazunori Yamauchi had been developing for 5 years on the game, but it was worth it because Grand Turismo was sold 10,85 million times worldwide. This made Gran Turismo the best ever sold game by Playstation. The game scored on aggerate of 95% in The GameRanking what made the game the highest scoring race game till 2019 when Gran Turismo shut his servers. After that the game had 10 more game added to the series. The last one was gran Turismo 7 which was released on 4 march 2022.
