Information About The Game

On 23 October 2012 published Microsoft Studios the Race game Forza Horizon. Forza Horizon is a game developed by Playground Games. In the begin of the game you can choose between 2 starter cars: Chevrolet C8 Corvette Stingray and the Ford Bronco. On 30 September 2014, Playground games released Forza Horizon 2. On 27 September 2016, Forza Horizon 3. On 28 September 2018, Forza Horizon 4 and on 5 November 2021 they released Forza Horizon 5. Forza Horizon 5 Forza Horizon 5 is the fifth Forza Horizon title and the twelfth main instalment in the forza series. The game was released on 5 November 2021. The longest track on the game is the Goliath. When the game launched it had over 10 million players in the first week, which made it the biggest-ever launch for an Xbox Game Studios Game.
